Jim's Hobbies Overview

Jim's hobbies include Snowboarding, 3d Printing and Welding/Metal Fabrication. The First hobby snowboarding is an exhilerating experience of strapping an oversized skateboard to your feet and riding down a mountainside slope on snow. It's a fast adrenaline filled experience that will keep you surprisingly warm in the snow. 3D Printing is a small CNC machine that takes cartesian coordinate toolpaths and builds up a topographic object using a plastic extruder that operates much like a motorized hot glue gun. Welding and Metal Fabrication is the design shaping cutting and welding together of metal with the intent to create a functional mechanical part.
