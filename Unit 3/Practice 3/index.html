Favorite Hobby

I have been playing off and on for a couple of years. My friend was invited several years ago, and he didn't want to go by himself. After that day I was hooked, the technique that is required is very similar to play golf. Recently, I have been focused on improving my technique. This sport is a great stress reliever and it is great for exercise.
