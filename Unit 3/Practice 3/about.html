Favorite Hobby

Jim's hobbies include Snowboarding, metal fabrication and welding, 3d printing and cryptocurrencies. Jim has been doing metal fabrication for the purpose of developing automotive performance parts since high school. While Jim was stationed in Germany he learned to snowboard and has been an avid snowboarder since about 2003. During a training exercise in 2016, Jim shattered his left hand in six places. This required him to find a new hobby while it was healing. Jim picked up 3d printing. Jim enjoyed the process of 3d printing and even starting building his own 3d printers to include modifying the Marlin firmware and setting up a wifi print servor.
