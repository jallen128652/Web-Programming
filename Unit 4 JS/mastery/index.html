Jim's love of Dad Jokes

As a father of three, Jim loves a good Dad joke at the expense of his children. This page is dedicated to all the dad's that love to have fun with their kids.

The top 10

  1. The classic: "Dad I'm hungry." "Nice to meet you Hungry, I'm Dad."
  2. "I don't trust stairs. They're always up to something."
  3. "Dad, can you put the dog out?" "I didn't know it was on fire."
  4. "What's Forrest Gump's Facebook password? 1forest1."
  5. "Knock, knock. Who's there? A little old lady. A little old lady who? Hey, you can yodel!"
  6. "Air used to be free at the gas station. Now it's $1.50. You know why? Inflation."
  7. "I'll call you later." "Don't call me later, call me Dad!"
  8. "Why did the gym close down? It just didn't work out."
  9. "What did Yoda say when he saw himself in 4K? HDMI."
  10. "Did you get your haircut?" "No, I got them all cut."
