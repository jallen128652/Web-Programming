Jim's early Life

Jim was born on February 16th 1978 in Mount Holly Township NJ. When Jim was just 6 months old, his parents left military survice and moved to Southern California. Jim attended Quartz Hill High School and then promptly ran into troubles after high school. After that Jim moved to Las Vegas for a fresh start. While in Las Vegas Jim worked as a carwash manager for Terrible Herbst Oil Company and later as an Iron Worker. While Jim lived in Las Vegas, he had a pitbull puppy names Shovelhead. Just the best boy ever. Well long story short his apartment complex didn't like Shovelhead. While he was in between places to live, he boarded shovelhead with a friend till he could get another apartment. This "friend" left Shovelhead in the backyard and left town without telling Jim. By the time Jim found out, poor Shovelhead had been taken to a kill shelter by Animal Control and euthanized ;( This was a significant emotional event for Jim which lead him to leaving Las Vegas and joining the Army in July of 2001.

Jim's Military Career

Jim's first duty station was in Germany where he would meet his gorgeous wife Katrin. Jim and Kat have been together for 20 years as of Christmas day.

Jim went on to deploy to Iraq from Germany twice and then to Afghanistan. When Jim left Germany in 2012, he was a Sergeant First Class. Jim's next duty station was Fort Hood in Texas. While he was stationed at Fort Hood he served as a Platoon Sergeant and later a First Sergeant. Jim deployed two more times while at Fort Hood, once to Kuwait and one final trip to Iraq. before retiring after 21 years as a Master Sergeant. Along the way Jim had 3 kids; Jesse, Laura and Eryn. Finally Jim retired and started persuing school full time which brings us to present day.
